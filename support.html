What is Fitargo?

FITARGO is the App to get your body to the maximum. With a simple, yet powerful interface we give you the power to create your specific, tailor made workouts and track your progress. To achieve this, FITARGO provides you with an extensive pack of free exercises selected and created by the best trainers in the field - all explained in detail with pictures and instructions. It brings simplicity and productivity to your workout routines while being as easy to use as your favorite music player. With FITARGO you can create your own workout-routines, try one of our unique, high-power pre set workouts or ask your personal fitness coach to input his training plan. With our free library of exercises you can specifically target separate body areas and train right away: Abdominals, Abductors, Adductors, Biceps, Calves, Chest, Glutes, Hamstrings, Lats, Lowerback, Middleback, Neck, Quadriceps, Shoulders, Traps, Triceps - Get started now!

Ho do I restore my purchases?
